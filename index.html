What is CGPA?

Well CGPA means Cumulative Grade Point Average, many national university students don't know how to calculate CGPA so i'm going to explain how NU CGPA calculation system works. Down below i have attached a grading system explanation image.

NU grading system

In this picture you can see more then 80 is A+, more then 75 is A, more then 70 is A-, more then 65 is B+, more then 60 is B, more then 55 is B-, more then 50 is C+, more then 45 is C, more then 40 is D, and less then 40 is F.
